Mathematical Formulation

The APG-DST optimizer minimizes: f(β) + Σ λj(k) |βj|
where the adaptive thresholds are: λj(k) = λ₀ · wj(k) · schedule(k)
with weights: wj(k) = 1 / (|βj| + ε)γ (subdifferential-informed)
and schedule: schedule(k) = max(τmin, 1 - k/K) (iteration-dependent decay)
